    Introduction
    Hi! I’m Annalise. I’ve taken a bit of an untraditional path to product design. My curiosity for human behavior took me to UC Berkeley where I completed my degree in cognitive science. I kept finding myself working within creative, design-oriented spaces. Now, with the support of a Fulbright award, I’m currently based in Milan, working on my master’s degree in Integrated Product Design at Politecnico di Milano. If you’re interested in my work, feel free to send me an email and we can schedule a time to chat.
