    Introduction
    I’m Annalise! I’m a student at UC Berkeley studying Cognitive Science and Design Innovation, specializing in product research and development.
    My talents lie in the creation of both physical and digital assets. I have worked extensively with the Adobe suite to put out graphics for wide consumption. My experience in research and journalism allows for key insights into the processes that go behind understanding the populations we serve with the products that we create. My strengths include putting out quality content while meeting deadlines, working with teams to ideate creative solutions, and producing media that strives to change perspectives.
    My favorite weekend activity is exploring the art and culture of the Bay Area! Catch me at Saturday morning figure drawing in Kroeber Hall, enjoying Greek Theater concerts from Cheapskate Hill, or at Cheeseboard splitting a pizza and listening to jazz with other Super Users.
