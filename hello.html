    • Hello.

    Hello! I’m Annalise. I’m currently a fourth year student at UC Berkeley studying Cognitive Science and Design Innovation, specializing in product research and development. I currently work as a Graphic Design intern at the UC Berkeley Library Communications office.

    In addition to my graphic design work, I serve as a research assistant in the Whitney Lab for Perception and Action , designing and running experiments with Allison Yamanashi-Leib that look at ensemble perception. Over the summer, I’m working with the lab to teach high school students how to use MatLab for psychology research.

    When I’m not at Berkeley Way west, I’m just up Hearst Ave, in the basement of Sutardja Dai Hall, volunteering as a Super User in the Invention Lab, one of Berkeley’s makerspaces.

    My favorite weekend activity is exploring the art and culture of the Bay Area! Catch me at Saturday morning figure drawing in Kroeber Hall, enjoying Greek Theater concerts from Cheapskate Hill, or at Cheeseboard splitting a pizza and listening to jazz with other Super Users.
